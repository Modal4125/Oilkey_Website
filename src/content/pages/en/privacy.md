Oilkey Corporation ("we") handles personal information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information we collect

Through our contact form, you provide your name, company, department, email address, phone number, and the content of your inquiry.

## 2. How we use it

We use this information only to:

- Respond to your inquiry
- Provide quotations, catalogues, and other requested materials
- Communicate regarding business transactions

## 3. Disclosure to third parties

We do not provide personal information to third parties without your consent, except where required by law.

## 4. Service providers

Contact form submissions are delivered using an external email service. We require service providers to manage personal information appropriately.

## 5. Analytics

This site uses cookieless analytics to improve the site. No personally identifying information is collected.

## 6. Requests for disclosure, correction, or deletion

We will respond promptly to requests to disclose, correct, or delete your personal information after verifying your identity.
